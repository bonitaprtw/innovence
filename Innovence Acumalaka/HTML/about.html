Company Overview

Innovence is a entrepreneurship learning platform built to empower the next generation of creative, competitive, and resilient young innovators.

We believe every idea holds the potential to shape the future. Our programs are designed to guide students through hands-on entrepreneurship learning, one-on-one mentorship, communication and pitching training, and complete preparation for business competitions.

Vision

To be the accelerator of young innovators to think creatively, act courageously, and transform their ideas into sustainable innovation and business value—especially within the landscape of competitive business challenges.

Mision

  • To build a supportive ecosystem where young innovators can explore, experiment, and bring their ideas to life with confidence.
  • To deliver practical, project-based learning experiences that bridge theoretical knowledge with real execution, preparing students for diverse business competitions until win.
  • To connect learners with certified mentors, industry practitioners, and a collaborative community that promotes mutual growth and continuous excellence.
  • To cultivate a culture of creativity, resilience, and action-oriented thinking as the foundation for Indonesia's future entrepreneurs.
  • To strengthen students’ communication and pitching skills, enabling them to present ideas with clarity, confidence, and strong stage presence.
